What's new in Ladder V2
  • The ladder now has player stats.
  • You can now view the list of matches a player has played by clicking on the player name on the stats page.
  • The ladder has a new GUI.
  • You can now add a "headline" that announces the results of a match.
    • The headline is displayed in the "Recent News" section of the main ladder page.
    • A default headline, which you can edit, is generated from the result of the match.
    • If you prefer not to have your results listed in "Recent News", you can clear the generated headline while entering the match results.
  • You can now cancel a challenge if you are the challenger. If you cancel a challenge, the result of the match will be recorded as "cancelled".
  • Handicaps: You can now optionally participate in handicaps by offering a handicap or challenging another player's handicap offer.
    • By default, you are not participating in handicaps until you offer a handicap or challenge a handicap offer.
    • If the challenger wins a handicap match, the challenger moves up one position in the ladder, the challengee moves down one position in the ladder. However, no player can move up over a player who does not participate in the handicap matches.
    • A handicap offer is available only until someone challenges it.
    • You can offer multiple handicaps and remove them when you wish.
